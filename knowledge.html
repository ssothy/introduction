What I Know

I feel like I've been practicing web design ever since middle school from the Myspace days. I even kept taking web design classes in high school and I just can't remember a time when I wasn't making websites. Taking web design at NOVA basically reinforced what I knew, gave me more confidence with css, and taught me how to make a sectioned website without using tables because I remember that's the only way I knew how to do it in high school. I think my biggest strength is my eye for aesthetics. I've always had an eye for sleek looks with a usage of harmonious color themes. My weakness is that I think I'm pretty lazy or that I haven't fully integrated html knowledge in my brain. I rely on w3schools too much I think.
